OUR PROJECT'S MOTIVATION

Originating from the question of why Digital Humanities remains unpopular and challenging in Vietnam compared to its current state in the world, DHVN is a project founded by a group of students from Fulbright University Vietnam in 2022 to research the topic and create solutions for relevant concerns. This website represents itself as a technological solution that can offer knowledge and resources that are friendly for beginners and networking capabilities to promote collaboration between learners as well as practitioners of digital humanities studies and people from other fields.
